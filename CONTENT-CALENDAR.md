## Content Quality Standards

### Minimum Requirements per Post

- **Word count:** 1,500 words minimum (pillar content: 2,500+)
- **Structure:** H1 + 4-8 H2 sections + H3 subsections where needed
- **Internal links:** Minimum 3 links to other blog posts + 1 link to a feature page
- **External citations:** Minimum 2 cited sources (industry reports, RFCs, standards)
- **Visuals:** At least 1 diagram, table, or comparison chart per post
- **CTA:** Contextual call-to-action (not just "try ooshare.io")
- **First-person experience:** At least one "we" statement per post showing real experience
- **AI citability:** Lead each major section with a direct answer in 40-60 words

### SEO Checklist per Post

- [ ] Unique title tag (<60 chars)
- [ ] Unique meta description (<155 chars)
- [ ] Target keyword in H1 and first 100 words
- [ ] Keyword in at least 2 H2 headings
- [ ] Alt text on all images
- [ ] Internal links (3+ per post)
- [ ] External citations (2+ per post)
- [ ] BlogPosting schema with correct dates
- [ ] Unique OG tags (title, description, image)
- [ ] Added to sitemap.xml

### Translation Priority

Expand to all 6 languages for high-traffic posts. Priority order:
1. English (primary)
2. Spanish (large search market)
3. Portuguese (growing market)
4. Chinese (technical audience)
5. Hindi (emerging market)
